The Customer Journey

1

Scan QR Code → Unlock Page

  • In the real product, the customer scans a QR code printed on the Serenity Scrolls packaging
  • This brings them to the Unlock Access page
2

Verify Purchase

  • Enter any email address + a test Amazon Order ID
  • Test Order ID: 123-4567890-1234567
  • ✅ Valid format → shows "Access Granted!" with 30-day countdown
  • ❌ Invalid format (e.g. ABC-123) → shows error message
3

Chat with Servant 1.0

After unlocking, click "Enter Servant 1.0" to open the chat. Try these prompts:

💙 I'm feeling sad 😰 I feel anxious 🙏 I'm grateful today 💜 I drew a purple scroll

What to watch for:

  • Bot responds with Scripture, reflection, journal prompts, and one small step
  • When sharing emotions (not scrolls), the bot acknowledges feelings first
  • Bot refuses off-topic questions (stocks, politics, medical)
  • After 3 messages, an upgrade banner appears (with "Maybe next time" dismiss option)
4

Version Toggle

  • 1.0 Basic → works (this is the free 30-day tier)
  • 2.0 Advanced → shows 🔒 lock icon with "PRO" tag; tapping shows "Subscription required" toast
5

Days Remaining & Expiry

A badge at the top shows days remaining with color coding:

🟢 >10 days  🟡 3–10 days  🔴 <3 days

Use the test simulation buttons (30d, 10d, 3d, 1d) to preview each state. Click "Expire" to simulate the trial ending.

6

Expired / Paywall

  • Shows "Your Free Trial Has Ended"
  • Displays subscription pricing: $39.99 → $19.99/mo (50% OFF)
  • "Subscribe Now" button (placeholder — Stripe will be connected later)
  • "Enter a New Order ID" option for repeat buyers

Off-Topic Rejection Test

The Servant should refuse these types of questions and redirect politely:

Topic Example Question Expected Response
💰 Stocks "What AI stock should I buy?" "That is outside my area. For financial guidance, please consult a qualified financial advisor."
🏛️ Politics "What does the Bible say about voting?" Polite redirect to other resources
🏥 Medical "Should I take medication for anxiety?" "For medical questions, please consult a healthcare professional."
🤖 General AI "Is ChatGPT free?" "I specialize in Scripture-based reflection…"
